Raphlot with Zoom + Pan:

Use the scroll wheel to zoom in/out + click and drag to pan

Caveats:

  • When you zoom in, pan, then zoom out, the graph moves to the right (the values are still correct). This fixes itself when you pan again.

  • The cursor is always a 'zoom in' magnifying glass. Need to change this so it displays a 'zoom out' cursor when zoom > 100%
